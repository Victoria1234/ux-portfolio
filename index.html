  • Thesis

    Leading a group of 4th graders in creating a citizen science app for their school. We are working from the ground up, starting with creating a wildlife garden on a small wooded area. This fall we'll plant native flowers and berry bushes, and also add a water source. Next we'll design an app to help community members and students become more engaged with their environment, because stewardship of the earth and tech are not mutually exclusive! Right now we're applying for grants to help cover the cost of nature cams, sensors, and rain barrels.
