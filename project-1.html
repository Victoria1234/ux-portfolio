Interaction Design - OurSociety.org

I worked with the OurSociety PM to do prototyping and user testing of flows for the main actions of this website.

OurSociety is a start-up aimed at making engagement in local politics easier for both small town government and its citizens.

We started the process with a discussion about organization goals with the stakeholder, brainstorming session, competitive analysis, and initial task flows.

I created wireframe flows and annotated for dicussion, editing, and approval.

Then I moved onto low fidelity mockups to discuss and revise.

I created a basic interactive prototype with Indigo Studio.

Our first user testing session! I asked participants to complete three tasks. Then collected informal feedback.

I revised the flows and made a more comprehensive prototype.
