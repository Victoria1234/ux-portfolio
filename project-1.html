Citizen Science

I worked with one other designer on this field study to learn about how people use smartwatches. We started by each conducting three interviews of smartwatch users in their home or office. It was helpful to see their environment and the context in which they use their smartwatch. We asked them to demonstrate how and what they use their smartwatches for. We compared and analyzed out data which led to the creation of interview-based personas, key findings, task flows, a user journey, and use matrix all for a final presentation.
